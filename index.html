About me

I'm a data scientist and policy analyst, starting a PhD in Political Economy at the University of Chicago in Fall 2025.

I am currently a Research Engineer at the Mansueto Institute for Urban Innovation, at the University of Chicago, and an adjunct lecturer at the University of Illinois, Chicago.

I graduated from the MS in Computational Analysis and Public Policy at the University of Chicago in 2024, and prior to that I was at the Urban Informatics Lab at the Indian Institute for Human Settlements.
Throughout my career, I've been driven by the belief that technology, when thoughtfully applied, can be a powerful tool for public good. My experiences have spanned across data science, policy analysis, and urban informatics, each reinforcing my commitment to using data for societal improvement.
