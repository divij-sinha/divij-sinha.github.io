About

I'm a PhD student in Political Economy at the University of Chicago, and a data scientist and policy analyst. I'm broadly interested in studying India and the Global South, and am currently working on elections in India.

I am also a Research Engineer at the Mansueto Institute for Urban Innovation at the University of Chicago, and was previously an adjunct lecturer at the University of Illinois, Chicago.

I hold an MS in Computational Analysis and Public Policy from the University of Chicago (2024, Siebel Scholar), and a B.E. in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from BITS Pilani, Goa with a minor in Politics, Economics, and Philosophy. Prior to Chicago, I was a Research Associate at the Urban Informatics Lab at the Indian Institute for Human Settlements.
